Our Vision

Our vision is to build a holistic ecosystem that gives engineers of the future the complete arsenal to tackle real-world challenges in Robotics and Automation. Our content aims to engender the natural curiosity of our learners through our acclaimed approach of illuminating the simplicity in otherwise complex concepts, while also ingraining their mathematical rigor. We provide the opportunity for our learners to directly apply their knowledge in simplified versions of complex problems as means to reinforce their foundations. We aim to accelerate Robotics research by augmenting our content with the provision of a fully integrated software and hardware stack which we envision will be the standard of reliable, yet affordable Robotics Research in organizations globally. Moreover, we aim to familiarize our learners on interdisciplinary collaboration, equipping them with the skills needed to work alongside a diverse group of people with different skill-sets.

About Us

AllAboutRobotix as a one stop holistic platform to gain insights into the various topics associated with the field of Robotics. This is an attempt to bring to light all the important topics that are important in the field of robotics and computer science in general. AllAboutRobotix aims to take the complex and vast topics in robotics and technology and break them down for you in the simplest way possible. We introduce a platform that allows you to keep up with latest advancements in Robotics through:

  1. Beginner-friendly simplification of state-of-the-art research papers in Robotics.
  2. Summaries of workshops, tutorials and technical talks from the best Robotics conferences across the world.
  3. Interactive posts on fundamental, cutting edge technological concepts.

We will shortly be releasing courses that focused on different fields of Robotics, while allowing you to collaborate on interdisciplinary projects with other learners on our website. We are really excited to bring you this platform, we hope you are too!
